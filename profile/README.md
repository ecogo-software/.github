## The right hotel,   at the best rate — instantly

We've spent years on the parts of hotel search that other platforms still get wrong. Your agents feel it the moment they start using FareOS.

### One hotel, every rate side-by-side

Every provider names the same property a little differently. Without help, your agent ends up comparing four near-duplicates row by row. We've already done that work — 1.56 million provider listings cleaned up into 1.4 million unique hotels. Your agent sees one hotel, with every rate next to it, and picks the best.

### Photos and amenities, automatically

Most provider feeds give you a hotel name, an address, and very little else. We fill in hero photos, amenity tags, and place descriptions automatically — pulling from trusted public sources, only adding what's missing, never overwriting anything you've curated yourself.
### Instant search, always fresh

New hotels and new rates appear in search within seconds of going live. A nightly refresh makes sure nothing ever drifts out of date — so your agents never quote a price that no longer exists.
